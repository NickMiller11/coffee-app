Measure out your coffee beans and put them into the hopper.
Pour some hot water into your french press to heat it up.
Grind your coffee.
Dump the hot water and add the coffee grounds to the french press.
Put the weight scale and french press under the hot water pot faucet.
Add the hot water to the french press.
Use a chopstick to stir the coffee until all the grounds are saturated.
Put the french press lid on gently and set a time for 4 minutes.
At the end of 4 minutes, use the plunger to slowly push the grounds to the bottom.
When pouring, be careful to leave a little coffee in the french press so you can avoid pouring sediment into your mug.
