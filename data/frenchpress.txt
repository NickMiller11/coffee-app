Measure out your coffee beans and put them into the hopper.
Pour some hot water into your french press to heat it up.
Grind your coffee.
Dump the hot water and pour the coffee grounds in the french press.
Put the weight scale and french press under the hot water pot.
Add the hot water to the french press.
Use a chopstick to stir the coffee until all the grounds are saturated.
Put the french press lid on gently and set a timer for 4 minutes.
At the end of 4 minutes, slowly push the grounds to the bottom.
When pouring, be careful and avoid pouring sediment into your mug.
