Place the filter in the Chemex with multiple folds lined up against the spout.
Rinse the filter with hot water and heat the Chemex.
Grind your coffee beans.
Dump the rinse water and put the Chemex on the weight scale.
Pour the coffee grounds in the Chemex filter.
Add 100-150g of hot water to the coffee grounds.
Stir with a chopstick to make sure there are no dry clumps.
Add the remaining water and wait for it to brew.
